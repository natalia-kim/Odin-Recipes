Coffee Shampoo

Coffee contains properties that make it excellent for hair and scalp care.
It stimulates blood circulation and removes dead skin cells, preventing hair loss.
It also strengthens the hair, darkens the natural color a little, and provides a pleasant aroma.
If you dream of long and healthy hair, this homemade shampoo is exactly what you need.

You will need:

  • Neutral shampoo
  • 5 tsp of ground coffee (125 g)
  • 1 cup of water

How to prepare:

Use a shampoo that doesnâ€™t contain many components, the most neutral one that you have in the bathroom. Heat the water a little, without boiling it. Let it cool a little and mix the water with the coffee in the same cup. Stir to integrate. With the help of a funnel, pour the mixture into the shampoo bottle. Cover and shake well. Let the shampoo sit for 2 hours before using it.

How to use:

Apply the shampoo as you normally would, massaging the scalp in a circular motion.
Leave the product on for 5 minutes and rinse well.
You can use it 2 or 3 times a week.
