Coffee Body Scrub

Coffee is very good for fighting cellulite because, as we mentioned before, it stimulates blood circulation.
This helps remove excess fluid that accumulates in the connective tissue.
It takes a few extra minutes to include coffee in your beauty routine, but itâ€™s definitely worth the time.

You will need:

  • 5 tsp of ground coffee (125 g)
  • 3 tsp of wheat germ oil (75 g)
  • plastic wrap

How to prepare:

Mix both ingredients in a bowl until they form a thick paste.

How to use:

Apply the mixture to the areas of your body that you consider problematic (thighs, arms, buttocks, and belly).
Cover with plastic wrap for 30 minutes. Remove the plastic, massage the area in a circular motion for 5 minutes, and rinse with warm water.
Use it twice a week.
